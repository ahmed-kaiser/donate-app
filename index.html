Our Donate Plan

We work mostly on the fundamental needs in people's lives. Our aim is to reach as close to
the marginal area, where people need more help.

Food for a family

Food is an important element in sustaining life. Our aim is to make sure that poor people are getting food at least three times a day.

Shelter for a family

Everyone needs shelter. But everyone can't afford it. A shelter is part of a person's identity. So we want to make people's identities stronger. Also, so that they can live happy life.

Education for a child

To make people outer of poverty, education is the only key. So we want to give at least primary education to the child so they can sort their life on their own when they become older.

We are non-profit too

We have volunteers all over the county. Some of them are students, some job holders, and normal people,
they work on their own for happiness, not to get paid. And, we also work with other social
organization that works in this field.
